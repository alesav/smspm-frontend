Bulk SMS — sometimes called mass SMS or business SMS — is the practice of sending a large number of text messages simultaneously to a list of mobile phone numbers. Unlike one-to-one conversational SMS, bulk SMS is designed for one-to-many communication: promotional campaigns, transactional alerts, appointment reminders, OTP codes, and more.

## How Bulk SMS Works

When you send a bulk SMS campaign, your message travels through this chain:

1. **Your platform or API** — you compose the message and upload your recipient list
2. **SMS gateway** (like SMSPM) — authenticates, validates, and routes your request
3. **Mobile network operator** — delivers the message to the recipient's SIM card
4. **The recipient's handset** — the message appears in the native SMS inbox

This entire process typically takes **under 5 seconds** for most carriers worldwide.

## Common Use Cases

### Transactional SMS
- Order confirmations and shipping updates
- Two-factor authentication (2FA / OTP)
- Password resets
- Appointment reminders

### Promotional SMS
- Flash sales and discount codes
- Event invitations
- Loyalty program updates
- Product launch announcements

### Operational SMS
- Staff scheduling alerts
- Internal system notifications
- Delivery driver coordination

## Pricing: How Is Bulk SMS Billed?

Bulk SMS is priced **per message, per destination country**. Rates vary significantly by carrier and country — sending to Germany is different from sending to India. At SMSPM, we publish transparent per-country pricing with no hidden fees.

A typical campaign to 10,000 contacts in Estonia, for example, costs around **€150–180** at current rates — a fraction of what equivalent email marketing software licensing costs annually.

## What to Look for in a Bulk SMS Provider

- **Direct carrier connections** — fewer hops = faster delivery and higher reliability
- **Transparent per-country pricing** — avoid providers with opaque "credit" systems
- **Delivery reports** — know exactly which messages were delivered and when
- **REST API** — integrate SMS into your own apps and workflows
- **GDPR compliance** — especially important if messaging EU residents
- **Sender ID support** — use your brand name instead of a random number
