Sending SMS to a global audience doesn't have to be complicated. With SMSPM, you can reach contacts in 190+ countries through a single platform — with transparent, carrier-specific pricing so you always know what you're paying.

## Why Bulk SMS Works

SMS has an open rate above 90%, far outperforming email. Whether you're running a marketing campaign, sending OTPs, or delivering transactional alerts, SMS reaches your audience immediately — no app required.

## Setting Up a Multi-Country Campaign

1. **Upload your contact list** — CSV import supports phone numbers in any format. SMSPM normalizes them automatically.
2. **Choose your sender ID** — Use a branded alphanumeric sender where carriers allow it, or a numeric shortcode for markets that require it.
3. **Check per-country pricing** — Navigate to the Pricing page and filter by destination country. Prices are locked at send time.
4. **Compose your message** — Our SMS composer shows character count, segment count, and total cost in real time.
5. **Send or schedule** — Launch immediately or schedule for optimal local delivery times.

## Carrier-Specific Routing

Different carriers in the same country can have different delivery rates and costs. SMSPM routes each message through the optimal path and falls back automatically if a carrier is unavailable.

## Tips for High Deliverability

- Always use opt-in lists — purchased lists harm deliverability
- Personalize with merge tags `{first_name}` to improve engagement
- Keep messages under 160 characters (1 SMS segment) when possible
- Test with a small batch before sending to your full list
